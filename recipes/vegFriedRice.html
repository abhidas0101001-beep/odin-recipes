Veg Fried Rice

Description

A quick and flavorful rice dish with vegetables, perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan or wok.
  2. Add garlic and onion and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add vegetables and cook for 3–4 minutes.
  4. Add cooked rice and mix well.
  5. Pour soy sauce and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Stir fry for a few minutes and serve hot.
