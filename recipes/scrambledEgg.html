Scrambled egg

Description

A quick and protein-rich breakfast dish that is creamy and flavorful.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and add milk.
  2. Whisk until well combined.
  3. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour in the egg mixture.
  5. Stir gently while cooking until soft and fluffy.
  6. Season with salt and pepper and serve.
