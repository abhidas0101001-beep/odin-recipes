Fruit Smoothie

Description:

A refreshing and healthy drink made with fresh fruits and yogurt or milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed fruits (banana, strawberry, mango)
  • 1 cup milk or yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Steps

  1. Add fruits to a blender.
  2. Pour milk or yogurt into the blender.
  3. Add honey and ice cubes.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Pour into a glass and serve chilled.
