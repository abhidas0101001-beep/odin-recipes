Pancakes

Description

Soft and fluffy breakfast pancakes that are quick to make and perfect with syrup, fruits, or honey.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a pan with a little butter or oil.
  5. Pour batter and cook until bubbles appear, then flip.
  6. Cook the other side until golden and serve.
